# Mastering the game of Go with deep neural networks and tree search

**Authors:** David Silver, Aja Huang, Chris J. Maddison, Arthur Guez
**Year:** 2016
**Venue:** Nature
**DOI:** 10.1038/nature16961

## Abstract

The game of Go has long been viewed as the most challenging of classic games for AI. We introduce a new approach to computer Go using value networks and policy networks combined with Monte Carlo tree search. AlphaGo defeated the human European Go champion by 5 games to 0.
