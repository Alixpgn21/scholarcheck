# Exploring the Limits of Transfer Learning with a Unified Text-to-Text Transformer

**Authors:** Colin Raffel, Noam Shazeer, Adam Roberts, Katherine Lee
**Year:** 2020
**Venue:** JMLR
**DOI:** 10.48550/arxiv.1910.10683

## Abstract

We introduce a unified framework that converts all text-based language problems into a text-to-text format. By combining the insights from the NLP transfer learning literature, we survey the landscape of transfer learning techniques and introduce the T5 model achieving state-of-the-art on many benchmarks.
