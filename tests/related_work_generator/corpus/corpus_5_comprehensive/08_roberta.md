# RoBERTa: A Robustly Optimized BERT Pretraining Approach

**Authors:** Yinhan Liu, Myle Ott, Naman Goyal, Jingfei Du
**Year:** 2019
**Venue:** arXiv
**DOI:** 10.48550/arxiv.1907.11692

## Abstract

We present a replication study of BERT pretraining that carefully measures the impact of many key hyperparameters and training data size. We find that BERT was significantly undertrained, and can match or exceed the performance of every model published after it by training longer.
