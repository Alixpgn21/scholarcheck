# Proximal Policy Optimization Algorithms

**Authors:** John Schulman, Filip Wolski, Prafulla Dhariwal, Alec Radford, Oleg Klimov
**Year:** 2017
**Venue:** arXiv
**DOI:** 10.48550/arxiv.1707.06347

## Abstract

We propose a new family of policy gradient methods for reinforcement learning that alternate between sampling data and optimizing a surrogate objective function. PPO achieves the data efficiency and reliable performance of trust region methods, while being much simpler to implement and tune.
