# XLNet: Generalized Autoregressive Pretraining for Language Understanding

**Authors:** Zhilin Yang, Zihang Dai, Yiming Yang, Jaime Carbonell
**Year:** 2019
**Venue:** NeurIPS
**DOI:** 10.48550/arxiv.1906.08237

## Abstract

XLNet is a generalized autoregressive pretraining method that enables learning bidirectional contexts by maximizing the expected likelihood over all permutations of the factorization order. XLNet overcomes the limitations of BERT while outperforming it on 20 tasks.
