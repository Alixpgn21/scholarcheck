# Language Models are Few-Shot Learners

**Authors:** Tom Brown, Benjamin Mann, Nick Ryder, Melanie Subbiah, Jared Kaplan
**Year:** 2020
**Venue:** NeurIPS
**DOI:** 10.48550/arxiv.2005.14165

## Abstract

We demonstrate that scaling language models greatly improves task-agnostic, few-shot performance. GPT-3, with 175 billion parameters, achieves strong performance on many NLP datasets including translation, question-answering, and cloze tasks, often matching or surpassing fine-tuned models trained on specific datasets.
