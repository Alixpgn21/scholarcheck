# SQuAD: 100,000+ Questions for Machine Comprehension of Text

**Authors:** Pranav Rajpurkar, Jian Zhang, Konstantin Lopyrev, Percy Liang
**Year:** 2016
**Venue:** EMNLP
**DOI:** 10.18653/v1/d16-1264

## Abstract

We present Stanford Question Answering Dataset (SQuAD), a reading comprehension dataset of questions posed by crowdworkers on Wikipedia articles. We analyze the types of reasoning required to answer questions and find that many questions require multi-sentence reasoning.
