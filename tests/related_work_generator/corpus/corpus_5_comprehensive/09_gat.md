# Graph Attention Networks

**Authors:** Petar Velickovic, Guillem Cucurull, Arantxa Casanova, Adriana Romero
**Year:** 2018
**Venue:** ICLR
**DOI:** 10.48550/arxiv.1710.10903

## Abstract

We present graph attention networks, novel neural network architectures that operate on graph-structured data, leveraging masked self-attentional layers. We enable the model to implicitly assign different importances to different nodes in a neighborhood without requiring any kind of costly matrix operation.
