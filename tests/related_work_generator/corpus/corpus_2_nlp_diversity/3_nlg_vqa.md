# VQA: Visual Question Answering

**Authors:** Antol, Stanislaw, Aishwarya Agrawal, Marcus Johnson, Dhruv Batra, C. Lawrence Zitnick
**Year:** 2015
**Venue:** ICCV
**DOI:** 10.1109/ICCV.2015.279
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/1505.00468

## Abstract

We propose the task of free-form and open-ended Visual Question Answering (VQA). Given an image and a natural language question about the image, the task is to provide an accurate natural language answer. Mirroring real-world scenario, we allow for free-form human answers rather than multiple choice. In order to cutivate progress on this challenging new task, we introduce a large-scale VQA dataset containing over 0.7M QA pairs associated with 0.3M images. We establish baselines and provide an analysis of the task. Our experiments demonstrate that a deeper multimodal embedding coupled with attention mechanisms can push the boundaries of VQA performance.
