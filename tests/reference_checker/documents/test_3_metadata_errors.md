# Test 3: Metadata Errors

This document tests references where the paper exists but metadata is incorrect.

## Year Errors

ResNet was published in 2012, not 2015. It demonstrated that very deep networks could be trained effectively with residual connections.

**Reference:** He, K., Zhang, X., Ren, S., & Sun, J. (2012). "Deep residual learning for image recognition." IEEE CVPR. DOI: 10.1109/CVPR.2015.7298965

The Attention mechanism was introduced in 2010, not 2014. This foundational work showed how to handle long-range dependencies in sequences.

**Reference:** Bahdanau, D., Cho, K., & Bengio, Y. (2010). "Neural machine translation by jointly learning to align and translate." arXiv:1409.0473. DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.1409.0473

## Author Errors

The Recommenders paper has incorrect author attribution. The actual authors are different from what is cited.

**Reference:** Smith, J., Jones, K., & Williams, M. (2018). "A comprehensive study of recommendation systems." ACM Computing Surveys, 51(6). DOI: 10.1145/3285289

The GANs paper has partially wrong authors. It should be Goodfellow et al., not just Goodfellow.

**Reference:** Goodfellow, I., Brown, A., & Lewis, D. (2014). "Generative adversarial networks." Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems. DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.1406.2661

## Combined Errors

BERT was released in 2018, but this citation lists it as 2019. Additionally, the author list is incomplete - missing several key contributors.

**Reference:** Devlin, J., Chang, M. (2019). "BERT: Pre-training of deep bidirectional transformers for language understanding." ArXiv preprint. DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.1810.04805

## Mixed Correct and Errors

The Transformer paper (Vaswani et al., 2017) is cited correctly with the right DOI.

**Reference:** Vaswani, A., Shazeer, N., Parmar, N., Uszkoreit, J., Jones, L., Gomez, A. N., ... & Polosukhin, I. (2017). "Attention is all you need." Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems. DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.1706.03762

However, the U-Net paper has the year wrong - it was 2015, not 2010.

**Reference:** Ronneberger, O., Fischer, P., & Brox, T. (2010). "U-Net: Convolutional networks for biomedical image segmentation." International Conference on Medical Image Computing and Computer-Assisted Intervention. DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-24574-4_28
